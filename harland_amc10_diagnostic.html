    Honor Roll of Distinction: typically requires a score around 120+ (varies year-to-year, roughly top 1-2.5%).

    Important caveat: This estimate is based on a 15-question sample under simulated AMC pace, projected onto the full 25-question contest. Your actual score will also vary with the specific problems on the contest, your time management, and whether you leave hard problems blank (which earns 1.5 partial credit on the real AMC vs. 0 for a wrong guess).
