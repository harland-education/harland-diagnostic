Early Math Check · Kindergarten to Grade 2

A short math check for your child

This is a gentle placement check that helps us understand what your child already knows in early math, so we can start them in the right place. It is not a test to pass or fail, and there is no time limit. Most children finish in about twenty minutes.

For the grown-up helping

How to sit with your child

  • Sit together. You may read each question and the answer choices aloud to your child. Some questions have a picture; let your child look at it as long as they like.
  • Let your child choose the answer themselves. It is fine to re-read a question, but please do not point to an answer or hint which one is right.
  • If your child does not know, that is useful information. Let them pick their best guess or the choice that feels right, and move on.
  • The questions gently get harder. It is completely normal for a young child to reach questions they have not learned yet. Simply keep going as far as your child is comfortable.
The tap targets on each answer are large and clear. Your child can tap their own answer, or tell you and you tap it for them.
