Welcome to Your Science Diagnostic

This diagnostic measures your science ability across the core competencies that every science depends on, then goes deeper in the subjects you are studying. It begins with a short set of reasoning and data questions, then adapts to your answers to find your level. There are no grades and nothing to study for. Work carefully and do your best on each question.

  1. This diagnostic takes about 35 to 45 minutes. There is no time limit, so work at your own pace.
  2. Please do not use a calculator beyond simple arithmetic, and no notes, search engine, or outside help. We need to see your own thinking.
  3. The questions adapt to your answers, so different students see different questions.
  4. For each question, choose the single best answer. If you are unsure, choose the answer that seems most likely.
  5. You measure your reasoning skills across all the sciences, and your knowledge in the subjects you choose below.
